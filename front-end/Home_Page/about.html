Management Hub

Welcome to our Management Hub, your central control center for all things related to your Smart Apartment. Here, residents can access important information, manage account settings, and stay connected with the community. From rent payments to community events, the Management Hub is designed to streamline your experience and keep you informed.

Account Management Easily update personal information, set up automated billing, and manage lease details.
Community Calendar Stay in the loop with upcoming events, community meetings, and important announcements.
Documents and Forms Access lease agreements, community guidelines, and submit necessary forms conveniently.

Complaints Center

We understand that sometimes issues arise, and our Complaints Center is here to make problem-solving as efficient as possible. Residents can submit and track maintenance requests, report concerns, and receive updates on the resolution process. Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.

Maintenance Requests Submit and track maintenance issues with real-time updates on the status of your request.
Incident Reporting Report any concerns or incidents promptly, ensuring a swift response from our management team.
Feedback Portal Share your thoughts and suggestions to help us continuously improve your living experience.

Service Providers Portal

Our Service Providers Portal is designed to connect you with trusted professionals for any additional services you may need. Whether it's repairs, cleaning, or specialized services, our network of approved service providers ensures quality and reliability.

Approved Providers Explore a list of vetted service providers for various needs, from home repairs to cleaning services.
Service Requests Easily request additional services directly through the portal, streamlining the process for your convenience.
Ratings and Reviews Share your experiences and read reviews from fellow residents to make informed decisions about service providers.
